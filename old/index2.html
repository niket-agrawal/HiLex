HiLex !

HiLex stands for lexical proficiency test for Hindi. It is intended for cognitive researchers studying participants with Hindi (as native or non-native language) in an experimental setting. It consists of a simple un-speeded visual lexical decision task. This test will give you get an estimate of your Hindi vocabulary within 10 minutes.

It is quick, easy to administer, and free.
