HiLex !

HiLex is an acronym for the 'Hindi Lexical Proficiency Test'. It's a tool designed for cognitive researchers who want to study participants with Hindi as either their native or non-native language in an experimental setting. It uses a visual lexical decision task that's not timed to estimate an individual's Hindi vocabulary. By taking the test, participants can obtain an estimate of their Hindi vocabulary in just 5 minutes.

It is quick, easy to administer, and free.

Start the test     I'm an experimenter

Quick and Easy

On average, the HiLex takes about 5 minutes to complete. It comprises only 90 trials, making it a practically feasible addition to any psycholinguistic experiment.

Open Science

We invite everybody to use the HiLex for their research purposes. It is free to run on web with, with scores sent on your email id. It is made using jsPsych©.

Valid

In validation experiments, HiLex was evaluated as a measure of vocabulary knowledge on multiple psycholinguistic experimental paradigms.
