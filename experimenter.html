Use HiLex (online/ web-based) studies
  • Fill details on this page
  • Download the CSV file with roster and links for participants
  • Use either prefilled link; or send common link to participants with participant IDs
  • If you select custom-id, you need to supply different participant IDs to each subject
  • After participant finished the test, you'll get an email with trial level data
  • Participants will get email with just their calculated score (optional)
  • Participants can see their scores on last screen (optional)
  • You can redirect participants to any URL after HiLex is finised (optional; see callback URL feature)
  • Keep us updated with interesting findings
  • Email us if you need any help, see (contact us)
Use HiLex (offline/ lab-based) studies
  • Download the test in jsPsych package
  • Download the test with PsychoPy builder package (version: xxx.yyyy)
  • Download HiLex in MATLAB
  • Use with custom stimuli presentation softwares, download stimuli list with instructions and scoring
